Biblometric Analysis of Text Simplification Research

This website is a supplement to the paper titled "Biblometric Analysis to Text Simplification Research" and the aim of this website is to :-
  • Give access to interactive visualisations and Analysis
  • Serve as a supplement to the paper and provide better understanding about the obtained results.
Note
  • We recommend running website on Mozilla Firefox.
  • This website is only meant as a supplement to the results mentioned in the paper. For other information about methodology the reader should refer to the paper.
  • This website used d3js to render the interactive plots and some plots may take time to load.
RQ1: How the domain of TS has evolved over years ?
  • The development of the TS field is monitored by the number of papers published per year from 2012 to 2022 and these findings are demonstrated in figure below.
  • Overall, the number of publications on TS shows an increasing trend where the recent 2021 has been recorded as the year with the highest number of publications which was around 65.
  • The development of the TS field is monitored by the number of papers published per year from 2012 to 2022 and these findings are demonstrated in figure below. There is significant progress in TS during the years 2020 to 2021, where approximately 35 percent of the total publications have happened during these last two years.
  • However, it is noteworthy that this upward tendency has been interrupted by several drops in publication counts during 2015, 2017, and 2019 compared to their preceding years. This can be attributed to the fact that some conference occur in alternative years. The drop in 2022 can be attributed to the data being incomplete as number of publications could be under review at this point.
  • Among the popular years, 2016 is significant as the year with the highest growth rate.
  • In conclusion, the total growth in the number of publications explains that the field of text simplification has started to gain traction in the research community.
RQ2: Who contributes most to the research in the field of TS?
  • In this section, the publications are analyzed based on the engagement of the authors in TS research.
  • The citation count was considered as the criteria for recognizing the most influential authors
  • According to the findings, the ten authors are shown in the figure below who are the most influential authors in the domain.
  • One can observe that the top most author has more than 80k citations while most of the authors have citation counts between 20k-50k.
  • Moreover, by interacting with the figure one can observe that the affiliations of authors are diverse and there are few authors who work in Industry as well. This shows that people working in the field of TS belong to both industry and academia.
RQ3: Which countries contribute most to the TS research?
  • The awareness of the country-wise contribution on the research context is important for researchers to identify the locations where TS research is prominent.
  • Under this research question, the country-wise influence on TS was investigated by using the country of the first authors in publications.
  • However, as GS does not provide this information, we have manually extracted it from the authors’ affiliations of papers collected from 2012 to 2022.
  • Thevisualizes the country-wise contribution to TS publications through a map, where the values are displayed using a color scale; the more publications a country has, the darker the color is.
  • According to the findings, it is significant that the United States of America is the prominent contributor which owns around 45 percent of the total papers published by the top-five countries. More importantly, many important countries are from Europe, where the United Kingdom, Germany, France and Spain are the next four most contributors.
RQ4: What is the collaboration among researchers in the field of TS?
  • Collaboration analysis reveals how the research community in the field of TS is collaborating.
  • Each node represents the author and edge denotes the connection between them.
  • The graph below shows the overall collaboration between the authors and their co-authors and also displays information about their affiliations
  • If one interacts with the graph, one can observe that there are diverse affiliations of authors and some authors also have industry academia collaboration.

RQ6: What are the trends of TS research ?
  • The word cloud below visualizes the results of word cloud analysis conducted on the abstracts of publications.
  • This investigation revealed some important keywords about application domains, such as “medical", “machine translation", “comprehension", “linguistic", and “literacy".
  • This indicates that TS research has been applied in various domains ranging from medical to computer science and literacy.
  • We also performed topic modelling using LDA to understand themes in the field of TS.
  • The keywords and discovered themes are mentioned in the table below. The major themes revolved around Text simplification applications in Healthcare, Text Simplification metrics and Text Simplification applications in NLP
Keywords Theme
Medical, Health, disabilities Text Simplification applications in Healthcare
Simplicity, Grammaticality, semantic, accuracy Text Simplification metrics
Translation, Summarization, generation, supervised Text Simplifciation applications in NLP
Limitations
  • The data collection souce implements truncation, that means the abstracts and sometimes even the authors of the papers are truncated.
  • While generating citation graphs for temporal analysis, we were not able collect the authors full names because some authors do not have a google scholar profile, which would have resulted in disconnected graphs.
  • The dataset size is too small hence there are less themes, but with an increase dataset collected from other sources like web-science we can get better results.
